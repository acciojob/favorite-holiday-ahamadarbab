Dubai – My Favorite Holiday Destination


Dubai is a modern and luxurious city in the United Arab Emirates known for its futuristic architecture, world-class shopping, beautiful beaches, and exciting desert adventures. It offers a perfect combination of traditional culture and modern innovation. From towering skyscrapers to golden deserts, Dubai provides unforgettable experiences for travelers of all ages

  1. Burj Khalifa – the tallest building in the world with breathtaking views from the top.
  2. Palm Jumeirah – a man-made island shaped like a palm tree with luxury resorts.
  3. Dubai Mall – one of the largest shopping malls in the world with entertainment and an aquarium.
  4. Desert Safari – an exciting desert adventure including dune bashing and camel rides.
  5. Dubai Marina – a beautiful waterfront area with restaurants, boats, and nightlife.
  • Shawarma – a delicious street food made with grilled meat and wraps.
  • Al Harees – a traditional Emirati dish made with wheat and meat.
  • Luqaimat – sweet dumplings topped with date syrup.
  • Grilled Kebabs – flavorful and juicy meat cooked with spices.
  • Kunafa – a famous Middle Eastern dessert made with cheese and syrup.
